Where to buy my peruvian chip?

You can buy your peruvian chip on the Real Plaza Salaverry Mall. Inside you will find 2 stores: Movistar and Claro.
To buy a chip you need to have in mind that:

    1. Both stores opens at 10am
    2. Bring your original passport
    3. A chip costs aproximately 5 soles. Preferable pay in cash.
    4. To make a charge of internet credits to your phone. The prices is approximately 25 soles (per 2 weeks).

Tips for safety

It is advisable to consider these tips:

  • ✅ Try to use your phone just at the university, coffee shops, house, Mall, and apartment.
  • ✅ Try to walk outside with small cash just for your expenses.
  • ✅ Always pay in cash, so you can avoid card's clonation.
  • ✅ Get a photo of your passport in your phone. Keep your original passport at the house.
  • ✅ If you use a local bus, try to check your belongings always.
  • ✅ Once you get a taxi, sit back the taxi driver's seat.
  • ✅ It is preferable to use a reliable taxi service.
