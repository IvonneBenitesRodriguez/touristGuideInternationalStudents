Type of currency

The local currency 's name is the peruvian sol. In Peru we accept dollars also. If you go to a supermarket it is advisable to pay in soles, but if you do have dollars you can pay with a billet of maximum 20 dollars. There are exchange money houses where you can change your money. It is advisable to change your money inside this exchange money houses.One placed near to the house or the apartment is named western union, it is placed inside Real Plaza Salaverry Mall(at the basement).
